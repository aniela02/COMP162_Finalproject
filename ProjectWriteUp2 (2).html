Introduction¶

Link to dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jayaantanaath/student-habits-vs-academic-performance

Description of Dataset:

  • This synthetic dataset explores how various lifestyle habits influence academic performance in students. The dataset contains 16 variables and 1,000 observations, recording students' key habits and observations such as daily study hours, sleep duration, mental health ratings, exercise frequency, attendance percentage, and exam scores.

Important variables:

  • Study Hours per day
    • Average daily study time
  • Sleep Hours
    • Average daily sleep
  • Mental Health Rating
    • Self-assessed mental health rating between 1(very poor)-10(excellent)
  • Exercise Frequency
    • Times exercised per week
  • Attendance Percentage
    • Class attendance percentage (1-100)
  • Gender
    • Male/female/other
  • Exam Score
    • Final exam score (1-100)

Data Modifications:

  • Put study hours into labeled bins
  • Put mental health ratings into three labeled bins (low, medium, high)
  • Ended up dropping the student Id column, but not until after I accidentally one-hot encoded it.

y_pred = clf.predict(X_test)
y_pred_train = clf.predict(X_train)

print("training r2", metrics.r2_score(y_train, y_pred_train))
print("test r2", metrics.r2_score(y_test, y_pred))
print("RMSD", metrics.mean_squared_error(y_test, y_pred, squared=False))

print("Accuracy:", metrics.accuracy_score(y_test, y_pred))

labels = np.unique(y_test)
